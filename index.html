Our Community Rules

To ensure a positive environment, we ask all members to adhere to these guidelines. Violations may result in warnings, mutes, or bans.

General Rules

01

Be Respectful

Treat all members with respect. Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, and personal attacks are strictly prohibited.

General
02

No Spamming

Avoid excessive messages, repetitive content, or self-promotion that disrupts the chat flow.

General
03

English Only in Public Channels

To ensure all moderators can understand and enforce rules, please use English in public discussion channels.

General
04

Appropriate Profile Pictures & Names

Ensure your profile picture and username are appropriate and do not violate any other rules.

General Content
05

No Excessive Formatting or ASCII Art Spam

While creative uses of Discord's formatting are fun, avoid spamming large blocks of excessive bolding, italics, emojis, or intricate ASCII art that disrupt readability or flood the chat.

General Conduct

Community Rules

06

No NSFW Content

Do not post any Not Safe For Work (NSFW) content, including explicit, graphic, or offensive material, in any channel.

Community Content Safety
07

Protect Your Privacy

Do not share personal information about yourself or others (e.g., addresses, phone numbers, real names without consent).

Community Safety
08

No Advertising

Do not advertise other Discord servers, social media, or other communities without explicit moderator permission.

Community Conduct Content
09

No Cheating/Exploiting

Discussions or promotion of cheating, hacking, or exploiting in games or any system is strictly forbidden.

Community Conduct Safety
10

No Impersonation

Do not impersonate other users, staff, or public figures. This includes using similar names or profile pictures.

Community Conduct Safety
11

Keep Discussions Constructive

We encourage healthy debate and different viewpoints, but please ensure your contributions are constructive and respectful. Avoid circular arguments or bad-faith engagement.

Community Conduct
12

No Unsolicited DMs or Mass Mentions

Do not DM other members unless they have given explicit permission. Avoid mentioning large groups of users or roles without a clear and necessary reason.

Community Conduct
13

No Private Conversations in Public Channels

If you're having an extended one-on-one discussion that isn't relevant to the general chat or a channel's topic, please move it to DMs or a private thread/voice channel.

Community General

Voice Rules

14

Follow Channel Topics (Voice)

Keep conversations in voice channels relevant to the channel's designated topic. If your discussion is off-topic, please move to an appropriate channel.

Voice General
15

Respect Voice Chat Etiquette

When in voice channels, minimize background noise, avoid excessive mic popping, and use push-to-talk if needed. Do not play loud music or use disruptive soundboards.

Voice Conduct

Staff Rules

16

Follow Moderator Instructions

Always comply with instructions given by server administrators and moderators. Their decisions are final.

Staff Conduct
17

No Backseat Moderating

While helpful intentions are appreciated, please let the moderation team handle rule enforcement. Report rule violations to a moderator directly.

Staff Conduct
18

No Public Discussion of Moderation Actions

If you have concerns about a moderation decision, please address them privately with a moderator. Discussing disciplinary actions in public channels is prohibited.
