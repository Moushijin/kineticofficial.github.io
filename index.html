Community Guidelines

To foster a positive and thriving environment, we kindly request all members to follow these guidelines. Violations may result in warnings, mutes, or bans, depending on severity.

General Conduct

01

Show Respect

Treat all members with respect and kindness. Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, and personal attacks are strictly prohibited and will not be tolerated.

#General
02

No Excessive Spam

Avoid flooding channels with excessive messages, repetitive content, or disruptive self-promotion. Keep conversations concise and on-topic.

#General
03

English in Public Channels

To ensure effective moderation and community understanding, please communicate primarily in English within public discussion channels.

#General #Language
04

Appropriate Profiles

Ensure your profile picture, username, and custom status are appropriate and adhere to all server rules. Offensive or explicit content is not allowed.

#General #Content #Profiles
05

Mind Your Formatting

While creative formatting is welcome, avoid spamming with excessive bolding, italics, emojis, or intricate ASCII art that hinders readability or floods the chat.

#General #Conduct

Community Engagement

06

No NSFW Content

Do not post any Not Safe For Work (NSFW) content, including sexually explicit, graphically violent, or otherwise offensive material, in any channel.

#Community #Content #Safety
07

Protect Privacy

Do not share personal identifying information about yourself or others (e.g., addresses, phone numbers, real names without explicit consent).

#Community #Safety #Privacy
08

No Unauthorized Advertising

Do not advertise other Discord servers, social media platforms, or other communities without explicit permission from a server moderator.

#Community #Conduct #Advertising
09

No Cheating/Exploiting

Discussions or promotion of cheating, hacking, exploiting, or any form of unfair advantage in games or systems is strictly prohibited.

#Community #Conduct #Integrity
10

No Impersonation

Do not impersonate other users, staff members, or public figures. This includes using similar names, profile pictures, or deceptive language.

#Community #Conduct #Impersonation
11

Constructive Discussions

Engage in healthy debate and express different viewpoints respectfully. Avoid circular arguments, bad-faith engagement, or unproductive negativity.

#Community #Discussion
12

No Unsolicited DMs/Mentions

Do not send unsolicited Direct Messages to other members unless they have given you explicit permission. Avoid mass mentioning users or roles without a valid reason.

#Community #DMs #Mentions
13

Channel Relevance

Keep conversations relevant to the designated topic of each channel. For extended private discussions, please use DMs or a private thread/voice channel.

#Community #Channels

Voice Chat Etiquette

14

Voice Channel Topics

Ensure your conversations in voice channels align with the channel's intended topic. If your discussion veers off-topic, consider moving to a more suitable channel.

#Voice #General
15

Respectful Voice Usage

Minimize background noise, avoid excessive mic popping, and use push-to-talk if necessary. Do not play loud music, use disruptive soundboards, or engage in mic spamming.

#Voice #Conduct

Moderation & Staff

16

Follow Staff Instructions

Always comply with instructions and decisions given by server administrators and moderators. Their guidance is crucial for maintaining order and a positive environment.

#Staff #Moderation
17

No Backseat Moderating

While your assistance is valued, please allow the moderation team to handle rule enforcement. Report violations directly to a moderator rather than addressing them publicly.

#Staff #Moderation
18

Private Moderation Discussions

If you have concerns or questions regarding a moderation decision, please address them privately with a moderator. Public discussion of disciplinary actions is prohibited.

#Staff #Moderation
