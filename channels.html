Channel Guide

Navigate through the different channels in our server and understand their purpose. Find the right place for your discussions!

Server Information

#entrance

New members land here! Get acquainted with the server, find basic instructions, and react to gain access to other channels.

#annc

Official server announcements and important updates from the administration.

#news

Stay up-to-date with general news, game updates, and community events.

#gws

Participate in exciting giveaways for various rewards and prizes!

Tryouts

#tryout

The main channel for applying to join our competitive teams. Follow the instructions carefully.

#rules

Specific rules and requirements for participating in tryouts.

#results

Find out the results of tryout applications here.

Community

#general

The primary chat channel for all members to discuss anything and everything!

#media

Share your cool gaming clips, screenshots, artwork, and other media with the community.

#suggestions

Have an idea to improve the server or community? Drop it here!

#cmds

A dedicated channel for interacting with server bots and using commands.

Scrims

#scrims-req

Request or arrange scrims (practice matches) with other teams.

#scrim-results

Post and view the results of completed scrim matches.

#scrim-recordings

Share and watch recordings or VODs of scrims for analysis and highlights.
