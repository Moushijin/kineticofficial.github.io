Channel Guide

Navigate our Discord server with ease! Here's a breakdown of our channels and their primary uses to help you find your way around.

Server Information

#entrance

This is the first channel new members see. Provides a quick overview and guides you on how to get started in the server. A must-read for everyone joining!

#Info #Welcome

#annc

Stay updated with important server announcements, official notices, and general news from the staff team. Only staff can post here.

#Announcements #Updates

#news

This channel is used to share specific server news, such as updates to our website, new features, or community milestones.

#News #ServerInfo

#gws

Participate in exciting giveaways and contests here for a chance to win various prizes and rewards!

#Giveaways #Contests

Tryouts

#tryout

This is where you can sign up or get information about ongoing player tryouts for our competitive teams.

#Recruitment #Tryouts

#rules

Find the specific rules and guidelines that apply to all tryout participants. Please read these carefully before signing up.

#TryoutRules #Guidelines

#results

Check this channel for the official results and announcements regarding completed tryouts.

#TryoutResults #Announcements

Community

#general

The main hub for all casual conversations, discussions, and socializing. Feel free to chat about anything within server rules.

#Chat #Social

#media

Share your favorite memes, funny images, GIFs, and other visual content here. Keep it appropriate and fun!

#MediaShare #Memes

#suggestions

Have an idea to improve the server? Found a bug? Let us know your constructive feedback and suggestions here!

#Feedback #Ideas

#cmds

Use this channel to interact with our various server bots. Keep bot commands here to prevent spam in other channels.

#Bots #Commands

Scrims

#scrims-req

This channel is for making scrim requests, finding opponents, or scheduling practice matches for your team.

#Scrims #LFG

#scrim-results

View the official results and scores from recent scrim matches. See how teams performed!

#Results #Scrims

#scrim-recordings

Find VODs (video on demand) and recordings of past scrim matches. Analyze gameplay or re-watch your favorite moments.

#Recordings #VODs

**Note:** Staff channels and other private channels are not listed here for security and privacy reasons.
