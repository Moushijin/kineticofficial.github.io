Channel Guide

Navigate our Discord server with ease! Here's a breakdown of our channels and their primary uses to help you find your way around.

Information & Announcements

#announcements

Stay updated with important server news, event announcements, major changes, and official updates from the staff team. Only staff can post here.

#Info #Updates

#rules

Read our server's community guidelines and rules to ensure a positive and respectful environment for everyone. Required reading for all members.

#Info #Guidelines

#welcome

A warm greeting for new members joining our server! Find basic info and introduce yourself to the community here.

#Info #NewMembers

#roles

Assign yourself various roles to gain access to specific channels, express your interests, or show off your game preferences.

#Info #Roles

General & Community Text Channels

#general-chat

The main hub for all casual conversations, discussions, and socializing. Feel free to chat about anything within server rules.

#General #Chat

#media-share

Share your favorite memes, funny images, GIFs, screenshots, and other visual content here. Keep it appropriate!

#Media #Memes

#off-topic

A channel for discussions that don't fit into other categories. Talk about your day, hobbies, or anything else that comes to mind.

#Off-Topic #Casual

#bot-commands

Use this channel to interact with our various server bots. Keep bot commands here to prevent spam in other channels.

#Bots #Commands

#suggestions

Have an idea to improve the server? Found a bug? Let us know your constructive feedback and suggestions here!

#Feedback #Ideas

Voice Channels

General Voice Chat

Hop in here to chill, chat, and hang out with other members. Perfect for casual voice conversations.

#Voice #Hangout

Gaming Voice Chat

Dedicated voice channel for LFG (looking for group) and in-game communication. Coordinate with your teammates here.

#Voice #Gaming

AFK Channel

If you're inactive in other voice channels for too long, you'll be automatically moved here. Join back when you're ready!

#Voice #AFK

Game-Specific Channels

#vl-discussion

Discuss strategies, tips, and everything related to Volleyball Legends. Share your best plays and ask for advice.

#Games #VL

#lfg-scrims

Looking for a team for scrims or just want to find players to queue with? Post your LFG requests here!

#Games #LFG

Staff & Moderation Channels

#mod-chat

A private channel for staff members to discuss moderation, server issues, and planning. (Only visible to staff).

#Staff #Private
